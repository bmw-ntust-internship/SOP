# Student Card Template — GitHub Project Card

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **How to use** (see [daily-log.md — Create Daily-log Card](https://github.com/bmw-ece-ntust/SOP/blob/master/daily-log.md#create-daily-log-card)):
>
> 1. Create your card on the lab GitHub Project board ([lab members](https://github.com/orgs/bmw-ece-ntust/projects/8) / [interns](https://github.com/orgs/bmw-ntust-internship/projects/4)).
> 2. **First bubble** (the card description): copy everything below the horizontal rule and fill it in. Keep it **edited in place** — it is your permanent profile, plan, and checklist status.
> 3. **Every following comment bubble**: one daily-log post per day, in the exact [daily-log.md — Formatting Standards](https://github.com/bmw-ece-ntust/SOP/blob/master/daily-log.md#formatting-standards) structure.
>
> **Checklist rules:**
>
> - Every checklist section is a `##` header **with its deadline**: `## <Title> (DL: yyyy/mm/dd)`.
> - Every checklist item carries its own deadline `(DL: yyyy/mm/dd)` and, once done, an [evidence link](https://github.com/bmw-ece-ntust/SOP/blob/master/daily-log.md#evidence-links) pinned to a 7-digit commit hash.
> - Checklist IDs are fully numeric (SOP convention). The Oral Exam and Handover sections reuse the numbering of [leaving-procedure.md](https://github.com/bmw-ece-ntust/SOP/blob/master/leaving-procedure.md) — the SOP stays the source of truth for item definitions; the card tracks their status.
